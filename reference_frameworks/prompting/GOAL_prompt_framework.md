# GOAL Prompt Framework — Avalon Escapes

> The official prompt structure for all AI-assisted content, planning, and strategy tasks at Avalon Escapes.

---

## What Is GOAL?

GOAL is a structured prompt framework that ensures every AI request produces useful, brand-aligned, actionable output. It replaces vague, one-line prompts with context-rich, outcome-focused instructions.

| Letter | Meaning | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **G** | **Goal** | What outcome do you want? What should happen after the AI responds? |
| **O** | **Objective** | What does success look like? What specific criteria must the output meet? |
| **A** | **Assets** | What context, constraints, data, voice, examples, and references does the AI need? |
| **L** | **Layout** | What format, structure, tone, length, and output style do you want? |

---

## Why It Works

Weak prompts get generic output. GOAL prompts get Avalon-specific, professional output.

**Without GOAL:**
> "Write a caption for a Maldives Reel"

**With GOAL:**
> G: Drive DM inquiries from warm Instagram followers about our Maldives dive experience.
> O: A caption that creates curiosity about Fuvahmulah specifically, ends with a keyword CTA, and feels like Rafa speaking from personal experience — not a travel agency selling a product.
> A: Rafa is Brazilian, ocean-obsessed, and has dived Fuvahmulah multiple times. The account is @avalon.escapes. Voice: premium, personal, emotionally intelligent, not corporate. Reference pattern: curiosity-gap hook + philosophical shift + keyword CTA (Tier 2 structural replication from @noareserrunt and @gavinheeks).
> L: 100–140 words. Short punchy stanzas (1–2 lines each). Hook on line 1. No hashtags in caption body. End with: "Comment 'FUVA' for our full guide."

## Example Prompts

### Example 1 — Improve a Reel idea

```
🎯 GOAL
Generate a stronger, fully developed Reel concept for @avalon.escapes that drives DM inquiries about our Maldives trips.

📏 OBJECTIVE
- A hook under 10 words that stops the scroll
- A visual sequence (5–7 shots with timing)
- A 120-word caption in Avalon's voice
- A keyword CTA at the end
- Tier must be clearly stated (Tier 1 / Tier 2 / Tier 3)

🧰 ASSETS
Brand: Avalon Escapes (see default assets above).
Current idea: "Show the real Maldives vs the overwater bungalow version most tourists expect."
Format: Reel (60 seconds).
Destination: Maldives — specifically Fuvahmulah, tiger sharks, underwater footage.
Emotion target: awe + curiosity + FOMO.
Reference pattern: Curiosity-gap hook (Tier 2) — structure from @colinduthie and @noareserrunt.
Marketing skill: Copywriting (benefits over features, specificity over vagueness, AIDA arc).

🖼️ LAYOUT
Output:
1. Hook (1 line, max 8 words)
2. Visual sequence (timestamps, shots)
3. Caption (short punchy stanzas, 100–130 words)
4. CTA (keyword-based)
5. Tier label + brief note on what NOT to copy
```

## Prompt Quality Checklist

Before sending any GOAL prompt:

- [ ] **G** — Is the goal specific? (not "write something good" — "drive DM inquiries for Maldives trips")
- [ ] **O** — Is success measurable? (word count, format, specific action required)
- [ ] **A** — Did you include Avalon context? (brand voice, destination, founders, reference patterns)
- [ ] **L** — Did you specify the output format? (don't let the AI guess)
- [ ] Ethics — Does the prompt instruct the AI to adapt structures, not copy exact content?
