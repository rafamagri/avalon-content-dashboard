## 6. Environment

**Luxury travel trends:**
- Intentional, slow, and transformational travel over check-list tourism
- Boutique, off-the-beaten-path stays over chain hotels
- Personal agency relationships over OTAs (people trust human curation)
- Ocean and nature immersion as a wellness category
- Founder-led brands outperforming faceless brands

**Influencer formats on Instagram (2026):**
- Short-form Reels dominate discovery (algorithm pushes Reels over static posts)
- Carousels drive saves (saved posts boost organic reach)
- Philosophical travel essays outperform generic travel photography captions
- Keyword CTAs ("comment GUIDE") drive high engagement signals
- Founder-on-camera content builds trust faster than polished agency content

**Creator economy context:**
- Audiences are saturated by sponsored content and are hungry for authenticity
- Personal experience stories > generic destination promotion
- Small accounts with genuine voice outperform large accounts with polished but empty content
- Reference group: 12 accounts studied — formats work regardless of follower count when structure is right

**AI tools in use:**

- Apify (Instagram data collection — used sparingly, credits cost money)
- Streamlit (local dashboard)

## 8. Preferences

**How we communicate:**
- Direct, warm, personal — Rafa or Sofia speaking, not "the Avalon team"
- Short sentences. Punchy stanzas. Emotional precision over flowery description.
- Specific over superlative — "the silence underwater at Fuvahmulah at 6am" beats "an unforgettable experience"

**How we sell:**
- Show the experience before selling the product
- Let curiosity do the heavy lifting — never hard sell in Instagram content
- Use keyword CTAs (comment/DM) not website links as primary conversion mechanism

**How we plan content:**
- Mix of formats each week: Reels (discovery), carousels (saves), photos (warmth), stories (connection)
- Balance of pillars: Ocean/Adventure, Destinations, Founder Story, Philosophy, Travel Guide
- Reference viral patterns but always adapt to Avalon's voice and destinations

**How we use AI:**
- AI assists, Rafa and Sofia decide
- Never let AI replace founder voice — only strengthen it
- Use GOAL prompts for all significant AI requests
- Run the content simulator before posting important content
