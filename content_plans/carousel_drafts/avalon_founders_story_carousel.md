# Carousel Draft: Rafa + Sofia — Why Avalon Exists

**Theme:** Founders story / why Avalon was created
**Purpose:** Trust + emotional connection + brand humanization
**Based on:** Avalon's "Two women. Two cultures. One mission." carousel

---

## Carousel Brief

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Objective** | Introduce Rafa and Sofia, explain the origin of Avalon, build deep trust with new and existing followers |
| **Target emotion** | Recognition ("they understand what travel can do") → trust → admiration → desire to work with them |
| **Audience** | New followers + warm audience considering booking |
| **Format** | Carousel, 7 slides |
| **Priority** | Avalon Brand System + Own Post References |
| **Viral/save angle** | High comment and share potential — personal story content drives conversation |

## Brand Compliance Notes
- This carousel is based on Avalon's own published founders post — same structure, evolved copy
- Do not genericize the personal quotes — they should sound exactly like Rafa and Sofia
- Slide 3 and 4 body copy should be reviewed and approved by Rafa/Sofia before publishing
- Circular headshots are essential — the face in the top-right corner is a trust signal, not decoration
- The collage on Slide 6 only works with real authorial travel photos — do not use stock
