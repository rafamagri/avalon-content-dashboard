## Color Direction
- Slides: Navy overlay over vibrant, warm Colombian photography
- The visual contrast between navy brand treatment and Colombia's natural warmth creates impact
- Let the photography provide the color — don't compete with blue-green water and golden mountains

---

## Brand Compliance Notes
- Sofia's Colombian identity is a genuine differentiator — name it directly (Slide 6)
- "The version most tourists never access" is the core value proposition — keep it specific
- Providencia is the surprise hero of this carousel — don't downplay it
- Avoid tourist-brochure descriptions — every line should feel like Sofia is telling you personally

## What to Improve Before Designing
- Source authorial Colombia photos (Providencia especially) — this carousel lives or dies on the imagery
- If Sofia has personal Providencia/Colombia photos, they are the priority
- Consider a bilingual version (Spanish captions for Colombian audience, English for international)
- Test a shorter 5-slide version: Hook → Providencia → Medellín → Sofia's angle → CTA
