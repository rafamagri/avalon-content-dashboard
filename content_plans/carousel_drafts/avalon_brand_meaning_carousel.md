# Carousel Draft: What Does Avalon Mean?

**Theme:** Brand meaning / mythology
**Purpose:** Brand awareness + emotional depth + DM inquiry
**Based on:** Avalon's own "¿Qué significa Avalon?" carousel + brand mythology
**Language:** English (with Spanish for soul moments — bilingual is authentic here)

---

## Carousel Brief

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Objective** | Re-introduce the brand mythology for English-speaking audience; build emotional depth; invite DM |
| **Target emotion** | Curiosity → wonder → belonging → desire to connect |
| **Audience** | New followers, people who've just discovered Avalon, anyone who wonders what "Avalon" means |
| **Format** | Carousel, 5 slides |
| **Priority** | Brand System (Tier 1) |
| **Viral/save angle** | High save potential — mythology content is searchable and shareable |

## Brand Compliance Notes
- This carousel builds on Avalon's published "¿Qué significa Avalon?" — a proven format
- Language updated to English for broader reach — maintain the same mythological depth
- "We design escapes" is the correct Avalon language (not "we book trips" or "we plan holidays")
- The emotion sequence (curiosity → mythology → personal meaning → philosophy → invitation) is the exact arc from the original post — proven to work
- Do not add destination hashtags here — this is a brand post, not a destination post

---

## Viral/Shareability Notes
- Etymology content performs well on saves — people return to it
- "The best places are not found. They are lived." is highly shareable as a standalone quote
- The mythology angle is distinctive — no other travel brand uses Celtic origin stories
- CTA is DM-based (not link in bio) — this is intentional, creates direct conversation

---

## What to Improve Before Designing
- Source a beautiful dark ocean or horizon photo for Slide 1 (no person, pure atmosphere)
- Ensure Slide 3 uses an authorial photo from Rafa or Sofia — stock ruins the personal feel
- Test the Slide 5 CTA button size — outlined button should not feel aggressive on mobile
- Consider adding a personal line from Rafa or Sofia in Slide 3 body copy for authenticity
